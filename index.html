What is NerveUs?

NerveUs is an online educational resource designed to inform patients about peripheral nerve injury and support their treatment by providing information on topics such as general anatomy, function, treatment, and recovery.

How do I use this resource?

If you're a patient....

Explore on your own by using the navigation bar above or selecting a module below. You can also navigate through each module chronologically by following the section buttons at the end of the page. Some pages may have interactive sliders or 3D models to help you explore and understand more complex topics such as nerve anatomy or your specific surgical treatment.

This resource is here to support YOUR healthcare journey, so browse freely, revisit sections of interest, and have conversations with your healthcare team about information you discover.

If you have any comments, feedback, or suggestions, we’d love to hear from you! Please fill out the form below and we’ll try to respond as soon as possible.

If you're a healthcare provider....

Feel free to distribute and share this resource with patients or other healthcare providers if you find it helpful! We strive to keep the information on this site accurate and up-to-date. Check back for chapter expansions in the future!
